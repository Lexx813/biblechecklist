What changes in onboarding

  • Shrink from 5 slides → 3 steps — less friction, same information density
  • Step 1 — Intent picker: "What do you want to do first?" with 3 choices. User picks → app navigates there. No more blank screen moment.
  • Step 2 — Daily goal: "How many chapters do you want to read per day?" (1–5 slider). Sets their reading target immediately. Takes 10 seconds.
  • Step 3 — Notifications: "Want a daily reminder?" — one tap to enable push. Protects the streak before it even starts.
  • Premium slide removed from onboarding — upgrade prompts are more effective contextually (after a real interaction) than as the last onboarding slide
