3 Approaches to Improving NWT Progress

All phases are live. You want gains across acquisition, retention, and monetization — the question is how to structure the work.

A

Full-Site Improvement Sprint

Tackle all three areas in one coordinated push. Landing page → onboarding → home dashboard → premium gates. Each piece reinforces the others: better landing = more signups, better onboarding = faster activation, better home = daily retention, better gates = more upgrades.

Landing page polish Onboarding flow "Today's Focus" widget Smarter upgrade prompts
⭐ Recommended — biggest overall lift, all three metrics move together
B

Conversion Funnel First

Focus exclusively on the visitor → signup → premium path. Tighten the landing page copy, redesign the onboarding to get users to their first win in under 2 minutes, and add contextual upgrade moments at the exact points where free users feel the ceiling.

Landing page CRO Onboarding activation Paywall timing
Best if signups are healthy but premium conversions feel low
C

Engagement Engine First

Make the app magnetic for people who've already signed up. A "Today's Focus" home widget surfaces the reading plan daily assignment. Better streak mechanics (milestone rewards, streak protection). Social proof loops (share progress, community activity). Retained users eventually convert — and they tell others.

Daily habit loop Streak milestones Progress sharing Community activity
Best if you have good signups but users go quiet after week 1
